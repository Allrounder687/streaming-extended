About

Streaming Extended v2.0.0

Automatically skip intros, credits, and recaps across multiple streaming platforms for a seamless viewing experience. Works on Netflix, Hotstar, Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, and Max by detecting skip buttons and automatically clicking them.

Features:

  • Multi-platform support (Netflix, Hotstar, Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, Max)
  • Smart platform-specific button detection
  • Keyboard shortcuts with customizable hotkeys
  • Comprehensive usage statistics and analytics
  • Configurable skip delays and timing
  • Auto switch to next episode
  • Visual notifications and feedback
  • Show tracking and viewing insights
